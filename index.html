About
Background & Direction

I graduated with a degree in Information Systems from Stellenbosch University, where I developed a strong foundation in how systems are designed, integrated, and managed. Since then I have been channelling that into cloud architecture, specifically AWS. I am currently building a portfolio of real AWS projects that cover networking, serverless, automation, and AI integration, all while working toward my AWS Solutions Architect certification. My goal is to design cloud systems that actually solve problems, not just pass exams.
